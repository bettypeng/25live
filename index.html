Scheduling Event Spaces at Brown:

Reimagining Brown University's 25Live
Event and Space Booking Website

Betty Peng, Neilly Ann-Tan, and Michael Markell

25Live is Brown's web application for booking rooms on campus for events. It is used by countless student groups to view space availability and book rooms. However, what should be a relatively easy and pain-free process becomes an invitation for frustration on the current 25Live website. The lack of intuitive design, ease of learning, efficiency, and memorability on these pages can be attributed to its poor user interface.
